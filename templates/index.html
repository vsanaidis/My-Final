What is Deree Plugged?

Deree Plugged is a hub for students of the American College of Greece. By hub we mean a place where the students can exchange opinions about courses and professors, communicate with each other, provide information about which courses they have taken this semester and a place where members of teams of Deree Athletics can communicate with eachother.

How did it start?

This website is part of my final project for ITC4214. I have had the idea about a "social media" specific for our college for a long time and with the knowledge from this course it is now feasible.

Purpose?

The purpose of Deree Plugged is for students to socialize, communicate with eachother, exchange ideas and get information about anything. This even includes uploading or downloading notes that students have taken to help anyone with their courses.
